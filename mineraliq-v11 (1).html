Sentinel-2 Optical
European Space Agency · Copernicus
● LIVE FREE
10-meter resolution multispectral imagery. 5-day revisit over Uganda. Visible, NIR, SWIR bands enable NDVI, water indices, mineral mapping. Accessed via Google Earth Engine + Copernicus Open Hub.
Powers in MineralIQ
  • Boundary breach detection — disturbance outside concession lines (Mubende ML-2204 case)
  • Mine footprint over time — extraction area proxy vs declared production
  • Tailings pond expansion — bright reflective patches tracked monthly
  • New haul roads — first sign of illegal mining before pits show
  • ASM cluster classification — small-pit vs industrial signature

Sentinel-1 Radar (SAR)
European Space Agency · Copernicus
● LIVE FREE
C-band synthetic aperture radar. Sees through cloud cover — critical for Uganda where 60-70% of days are cloudy. InSAR detects ground movement of millimetres.
Powers in MineralIQ

  • Slope stability — open-pit wall deformation
  • Tailings dam deformation — pre-failure warning (Brumadinho-style)
  • All-weather monitoring — rainy-season operational continuity
